While waiting for the second Perform Europe call to open in December 2023, discover the 19 projects supported by the first Perform Europe Edition (2021-2022).

With more than 250 presentations of over 45 artistic works from all performing arts disciplines across 27 Creative Europe countries and the UK, these 19 projects led by more than 85 performing arts partners tested innovative touring and distribution schemes from January to June 2022 with one common goal: Redefining how performing art works can reach audiences in a fairer, more inclusive, and more sustainable way.
