2021-22 projects

The first Perform Europe edition (2021-2022) supported 19 diverse, sustainable, and innovative Perform Europe partnerships selected by the Perform Europe Jury, with a total budget of XXXX.  With more than 250 presentations of over 45 artistic works from all performing arts disciplines across 27 Creative Europe countries and the UK, these 19 projects led by more than 85 performing arts partners tested innovative touring and distribution schemes from January to June 2022 with one common goal: redefining how performing art works can reach audiences in a fairer, more inclusive, and more sustainable way.
